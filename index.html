📸 Online Photo Exhibition – “35 Years: My OU Moment””

Step into an evocative visual journey that celebrates 35 years of growth, connection, and transformation at Ho Chi Minh City Open University. The “35 Years: My OU Moment” online photo exhibition invites you to relive precious memories through a curated collection of heartfelt photographs submitted by generations of students and alumni.

🖼️ These images — from the quiet beauty of familiar campus corners 🏫 to the vibrant energy of student life — capture authentic moments of learning, friendship, and personal growth.

🎉Each photograph is more than a still frame — it tells a story of perseverance, inspiration, and the enduring OU spirit that has shaped lives over decades.

🌿Whether it’s a candid shot from graduation day, a snapshot of late-night study sessions, or a joyful moment with classmates, every photo speaks to the shared experience of belonging to a community that values education, resilience, and the journey of self-discovery.

❤️We invite you to explore this meaningful archive, feel the emotions that echo through time ⏳, and relive the OU story through the unique lens of those who have lived, learned, and loved within its embrace.
